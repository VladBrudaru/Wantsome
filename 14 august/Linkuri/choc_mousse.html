Caramelia chocolate mousse

What you need:

  • 600 g Caramelia chocolate
  • 450 ml Whipping cream
  • 100 ml Double cream
  • 100 ml Milk
  • 60 g Egg yolk
  • 40 g Sugar

Method:

Melt the chocolate over bain marie.

Separately whisk the egg yolk and the sugar.

In a pan bring the milk and the double cream to boil,pour over the egg mix and bring it to 84 C.

Whisk the whipping cream until soft peaks.

Pour the cream anglaise over the melted chocolate,mix well.

Fold the chocolate into the whipped cream.

Let it set.
