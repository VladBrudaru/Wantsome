New York Cheesecake

What you need:

  • 240 g digestive biscuits
  • 100 g butter
  • 300 ml double cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 tbs cornflour
  • 800 g cremecheese
  • vanilla extract
  • 400 g soured cream
  • 240 g sugar

Crumble the biscuits and mix with butter,make the biscuit base.

Mix the creme cheese with sugar,add the soured cream and double cream and mix.

Add the eggs one by one,then add the cornflour,vanilla extract and a little bit of lime zest.

Cook 10 minutes at 220 C then 30 minutes at 110 C.
